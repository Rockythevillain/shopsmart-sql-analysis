# 🛒 ShopSmart Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
End-to-end SQL analysis of a fictional retail business 
built to demonstrate business analytics skills.

**Tool:** SQLite  
**Skills:** SQL (Joins, Aggregations, Subqueries, Window Functions)  
**Domain:** Retail / E-commerce

---

## Business Questions Answered
- What is the total revenue and average order value?
- Which product category generates the most revenue?
- Who are the top spending customers?
- How does revenue trend month over month?
- Which customers are at risk of churning?
- Which products need restocking?

---

## Key Findings
- 📊 Total Revenue: £1,178.82 across 5 months
- 🏆 Top Product: Wireless Headphones (Rank #1 in revenue)
- 📈 Biggest growth: January to February
- ⚠️ Sales momentum declining after Q1 — action needed
- 🔁 Only 10% repeat customer rate — loyalty programme recommended
